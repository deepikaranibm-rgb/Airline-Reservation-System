Frequently Asked Questions
How do I book a flight?
You can book flights through our website, mobile app, or by speaking to our AI assistant. Just tell us your departure and destination cities.
What's the baggage allowance?
Economy: 1 checked bag (23kg), 1 carry-on (7kg). Business: 2 checked bags (32kg each), 1 carry-on (7kg).
When can I check-in online?
Online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and closes 2 hours before flight time.
Can I cancel my flight?
Yes, cancellation policies vary by fare type. Flexible tickets offer full refunds up to 24 hours before departure.
